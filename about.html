While serving in WWII, my grandfather stopped by a small watch shop in Switzerland. He carefully picked out his very first Swiss made timepiece as a token of his time in Europe. He treasured that watch and everything it represented. As he wore it he reflected on the people and events that would forever impact his life. BRUNI is a trendsetting brand based off the idea that a luxury watch represents more than just a show stopping accessory. It’s an heirloom, a symbol, a memory that can to be worn every day. I’ve always loved watches. I believe that must be something I inherited from my grandfather.

My focus with BRUNI has been to develop a watch that is new and trendsetting but that doesn’t forget about the past that has made us. Life is a continuance, constantly moving forward, hour by hour, creating new memories. BRUNI watches are quality swiss movement watches that can be passed down from generation to generation representing those memories. Our Italian Leather straps are timeless and the minimalist watch face is a classic that will transcend the ages. Every watch we sell represents the passion I have for my family and for these beautiful timepieces.
